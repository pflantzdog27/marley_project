Corporate Profile

Marley Coffee®, trading symbol JAMN, is a coffee roaster, marketer and distribution company. The brand’s premium-roasted coffee and teas are being distributed to Grocery Retail, Online, Service/Hospitality, Office Coffee Service, and the Big Box Store industry. With the recognizable name of Rohan Marley, the son of Legendary Bob Marley, as Chairman of Marley Coffee, the company has been able to get into traditional sales channels effectively.

Marley Coffee’s gourmet product line is positioned for the significant trend and growing consumer awareness towards organic, fairly traded premium, and super premium specialty arabica coffees.

From the Marley’s Jamaica Blue Mountain® coffee farm, to partner farms in Africa, Central America and other top coffee-growing regions worldwide, Marley Coffee strives to support communities and the environment. Marley Coffee’s globally sourced beans are small batch roasted to ensure the utmost quality and freshness.
